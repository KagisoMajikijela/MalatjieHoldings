Welcome to TK Malatji Holdings Pty Ltd

TK Malatji Holdings Pty Ltd is a leading mining services provider specializing in: Drilling and Blasting, Loading and Hauling, Crushing and Screening, Mine Rehabilitation, and Bulk Earthworks.

The company is also a parent to three subsidiary entities and is currently operating at the Buffelsfontein West Open-cast Mine, under the management of Samancor. The project has been active for 3 months, proving our high capability, safety culture, and commitment to delivering on time.
