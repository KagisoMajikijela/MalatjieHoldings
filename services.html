Our Services

Comprehensive mining solutions for your operational needs

Complete Mining Solutions

TK Malatji Holdings offers a full spectrum of mining services, from initial drilling to final rehabilitation. Our integrated approach ensures seamless operations and maximum efficiency.

Drilling & Blasting

Precision Operations

Our on-site drilling division uses state-of-the-art drill rigs capable of achieving the required rock volumes to meet production targets. We operate with a highly skilled and experienced team ensuring safe and efficient blasting operations.

Key Features:

  • State-of-the-art drill rigs
  • Highly experienced blasting crew
  • Precise production planning
  • Strict safety protocols
  • Environmental compliance

Loading & Hauling

Efficient Material Movement

Our load and haul operations consist of a continuous cycle utilizing machinery such as:

Excavators
ADT (Articulated Dump Truck)
Front End Loader

This comprehensive fleet ensures a smooth and continuous mining cycle, maximizing productivity while maintaining safety standards.

Crushing & Screening

Material Processing

In our operations, we utilize state-of-the-art crushing machinery to process ROM material from the mining operation to meet the specific size requirements of our customers.

As necessary, we also screen the crushed material to adhere to the precise specifications outlined by the customer. Our screening and crushing processes are conducted with a focus on maintaining the highest level of quality and minimizing contamination and downtime of equipment.

Benefits:

  • Precise size specifications
  • Minimal contamination
  • Reduced equipment downtime
  • High-quality output

Mine Rehabilitation

Environmental Stewardship

Our rehabilitation team restores mined areas to stable and environmentally responsible conditions, ensuring compliance with legal and environmental standards. We believe in leaving the land in better condition than we found it.

Our Approach:

  • Soil stabilization
  • Revegetation programs
  • Erosion control
  • Water management
  • Long-term monitoring

Bulk Earthworks

Large-scale Earth Movement

We handle large-scale earth movement projects with precision and efficiency, supported by heavy-duty machinery and expert operators. Our earthworks services include:

  • Site preparation and clearing
  • Cut and fill operations
  • Grading and leveling
  • Drainage systems
  • Road construction
