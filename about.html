About Us

Leading the way in mining services excellence

Who We Are

TK Malatji Holdings Pty Ltd is a multidisciplinary mining services company delivering high-performance solutions through advanced machinery, skilled personnel, and disciplined safety practices. We are a parent company to three subsidiary entities, each contributing to our comprehensive service offering in the mining sector.

Our Current Operation

We are currently undertaking a significant project at the Buffelsfontein West Open-cast Mine, under the expert management of Samancor. This project has been operational for 3 months and stands as a testament to our company's capability and expertise in open-cast mining.

We work closely with Samancor and its team to deliver projects on time while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety that our industry demands.
